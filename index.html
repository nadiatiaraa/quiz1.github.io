home town

What's in my Hometown.

Bekasi City is one of the cities in the province of West Java, Indonesia. The name Bekasi comes from the word bagasasi which means the same as candrabaga written in the Tugu Inscription of the era of the Tarumanegara Kingdom, namely the name of the river that passes through this city. In 2020, the population of Bekasi city is 2,464,719 people. This city is part of the Metropolitan Jabodetabek and is a satellite city with the largest population in Indonesia. Currently, Bekasi City is developing into a residence for urbanites and an industrial center.

And these are some typical Bekasi foods that have a delicious and delicious taste on the tongue. Some of them are as below.

Special Food

Gabus Pucung

Gabus Pucung

For some people, the name cork pucung may sound foreign. You know, its existence is quite rare. In fact, this Betawi food is included in the list of Indonesian Intangible Cultural Heritage. Gabus pucung is actually a cork fish dish doused in a thick black sauce. The black color is produced from a mixture of kluwak (pucung), a kitchen spice that is often used to cook rawon.Details

Bekasi Cake

Kue Rangi dan Kue Pancong

Kue Rangi dan Kue Pancong

Both are traditional Betawi cuisines, cake pancong and cake rangi have an almost indistinguishable appearance. Yes, both have a semi-circle shape with brownish white skin. The way of making it is even similar, by burning the dough that has been put into the mold. Despite all these similarities, cake pancong and cake rangi actually have quite a striking difference, you know. Both in terms of taste, texture, to the way of presentation. The difference between the two traditional snacks will only be felt when we have eaten them. Details

Pastries Bekasi

Kembang Goyang

Kembang Goyang

The rocking flower cake is a traditional cake and has been passed down from generation to generation in Indonesia betawi community. There are family events or holidays. Cake Kembang Goyang is part of the Betawi community tradition. It's called bouncy flower because when the mold is dipped into the batter and released in a frying pan that contains a lot of oil, namely by shake it off. The mold is in the shape of a flower so that it is call it rocking flower. There are also prints that are shaped like stars. Details

Bir Pletok

This red drink that makes the body warm is purely made from spices. The rest is water and sugar. The combination of fragrant, spicy, and sweet spices plus the distinctive flavors of several other spices makes this Pletok beer taste very special.Details

Dodol Betawi

Dodol Betawi

Dodol Betawi is a legendary cake from the Bekasi Betawi kitchen whose manufacturing process is quite complicated. For the taste, there is no doubt, sweet and legit, coupled with the elasticity that makes our tongues addicted. Betawi people of Bekasi are usually served as special cakes for parties, Ramadan, Eid or Eid al-Adha and are very suitable for souvenirs.Details
